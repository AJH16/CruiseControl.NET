CruiseControl.NET : Support
This page last changed on Mar 29, 2004 by mroberts.

CruiseControl.NET is not a commercial piece of software, so we cannot guarantee support. However, we do have some ways to help you with issues you may have:

  • If you subscribe to the CCNet-user mailing list, and post any query you may have somebody will usually come back with a response fairly quickly. You can also use the CCNet-user mailing list to provide feedback (improvement ideas, feature requests, etc.)
  • The [CCNETCOMM:] is a public forum for discussing issues and features, along with providing a F.A.Q.
  • Finally, if you aren't able to use the public forums but would still like to discuss CruiseControl.NET, please email us at ccnet@thoughtworks.com and we'll try to answer as soon as we can.
